Packing Tips for Banff

Essential advice for your September proposal trip

September Weather in Banff

Temperatures range from 5-15°C (41-59°F). Pack layers! Bring warm clothes for evenings and lighter options for hiking during the day.

Mountain Hiking Essentials

Waterproof hiking boots, layers, rain jacket, daypack, snacks, and plenty of water. Weather changes quickly in the mountains!

Photography Gear

Don't forget extra batteries and memory cards! Cold weather drains batteries faster. Consider a tripod for those perfect proposal shots at Emerald Lake.

The Most Important Item

Double-check that ring! Keep it secure but accessible. Consider a small ring box for the actual moment at Emerald Lake.

Travel Documents

Passport, flight confirmations, hotel reservations, rental car details, and travel insurance. Keep digital and physical copies.

Canadian Border Crossing

Have your passport ready and declare any valuable items like the engagement ring if asked. No need to stress - you're legal!
